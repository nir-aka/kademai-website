Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

Akademai, Inc. ("Akademai," "we") operates akademai.ai, an academic AI platform for higher education institutions.

What we collect

When you visit our site, we and our advertising and analytics partners collect standard technical data, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited, through cookies and similar technologies. If you submit our Founding Partner form or contact us, we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email, institution, and role.

Cookies, analytics, and advertising

With your consent, we use the LinkedIn Insight Tag and Google Analytics, which set cookies to help us measure the effectiveness of our marketing and understand how visitors use our site. LinkedIn may associate this data with your LinkedIn profile. You can control these cookies through the consent banner on our site, your browser settings, or your LinkedIn settings. See LinkedIn's Cookie Policy and Google's Privacy Policy for details.

How we use your information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, evaluate partnership applications, improve our site, and measure our marketing. We do not sell your personal information.

International transfers

Our service providers, including LinkedIn and Google, may process data on servers located outside your country, including in the United States.

Data retention

We keep inquiry and application data for as long as needed for the purpose it was submitted, then delete it.

Children

Our site is directed at higher education professionals and is not intended for children under 18.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing us at contact@akademai.ai. EU/EEA residents have additional rights under the GDPR.
